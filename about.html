Our Mission

At DMV Math Circle, our mission is to foster a love for mathematics among students of all ages. We aim to provide engaging and challenging mathematical experiences through competitions, comprehensive handouts, and expert teaching. Our goal is to build a community of math enthusiasts who can collaborate, learn, and grow together.
